================================================================================
ARENA ROYALE - CARD IMAGE GENERATION GUIDE
================================================================================

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR ALL CARDS:
- Art Style: Mobile game card art, Clash Royale/Supercell inspired
- Vibrant colors, bold outlines, slightly cartoonish but detailed
- Characters should be expressive and dynamic
- Background: Transparent or simple gradient matching rarity color
- Resolution: High quality, suitable for game cards

RARITY COLOR SCHEMES:
- COMMON: Gray/Silver gradient (#7f8c8d to #5d6d7e)
- RARE: Orange/Gold gradient (#f39c12 to #d68910)
- EPIC: Purple gradient (#9b59b6 to #7d3c98)
- LEGENDARY: Red/Crimson gradient (#e74c3c to #c0392b)
- CHAMPION: Cyan/Teal gradient (#00bcd4 to #0097a7)
- EVOLUTION: Orange-Gold with sparkles (#ff6b35 to #f7931e with gold particles)

================================================================================
4x4 GRID LAYOUT EXPLANATION:
================================================================================
Each character gets 16 images in a 4x4 grid:

Row 1 (Top): IDLE POSES
[1] Front idle - facing camera, neutral stance
[2] Side idle - profile view, relaxed
[3] Back idle - rear view, standing
[4] 3/4 idle - angled view, slight turn

Row 2: ATTACK ANIMATIONS
[5] Attack windup - preparing to strike
[6] Attack mid-swing - weapon/ability in motion
[7] Attack impact - hitting the target
[8] Attack recovery - returning to stance

Row 3: SPECIAL ACTIONS
[9] Ability activation - using special power (glow effects)
[10] Running/moving - in motion
[11] Taking damage - hurt reaction
[12] Victory pose - celebrating

Row 4 (Bottom): EXPRESSIONS & EFFECTS
[13] Happy/Confident expression closeup
[14] Angry/Battle-ready expression closeup
[15] Special effect (projectile/spell they cast)
[16] Death/Defeat animation frame
